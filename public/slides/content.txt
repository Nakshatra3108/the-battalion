OBJECTIVE:
Secure the most **LOCKED** Control Battalions by the end of the operation.

HOW TO WIN:
1. **CONTROL** a Sector by occupying >50% of its slots.

SLIDE 3: CONTROL & LOCKING MECHANICS
================================================================================

                      üîí CORE MECHANIC: LOCKING üîí

This is the most important rule in the game!

1. FORMING CONTROL:
   As soon as you reach the required number (e.g., 6 in a Corner), 
   those 6 battalions become **LOCKED**.

2. LOCKED STATUS:
   ‚Ä¢ Locked units CANNOT be moved (Redeployed).
   ‚Ä¢ Locked units CANNOT be converted by enemies (mostly).
   ‚Ä¢ Locked units COUNT for your score.

3. UNLOCKED UNITS:
   Any units *above* the requirement remain Unlocked.
   ‚Ä¢ Unlocked units CAN be moved to other sectors.
   ‚Ä¢ Unlocked units protect your Control against removal.

SLIDE 7: REDEPLOYMENT RULES
================================================================================

                         ‚ÜîÔ∏è REDEPLOYMENT ‚ÜîÔ∏è

This is how you expand without spending money.

RULE 1: WHO CAN MOVE?
You can only move units OUT of a sector if you **CONTROL** it (>50%).
*(Having 3 units vs enemy's 2 is NOT enough! You need 6+ in a corner).*

RULE 2: WHAT CAN MOVE?
You can only move **UNLOCKED** units.
The units securing your control are stuck guarding it.

RULE 3: WHERE?
To any **ADJACENT** sector (connected by lines).

RULE 4: WHOSE UNITS?
You can move **ANY** unlocked unit - even enemies!
*Tactic: Force enemy units out of your sector into a useless one!*
